Who We Are

KNI Global Tours was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to share the incredible beauty, rich culture, and transformative experiences of Japan and India with travelers from around the world.

What started as a small team of passionate travel enthusiasts has grown into a trusted travel partner for thousands of adventurers from over 50 countries. Our deep connections in both Japan and India allow us to offer authentic, immersive experiences that go beyond typical tourist routes.

We believe travel has the power to transform lives, bridge cultures, and create lasting memories. Every itinerary we craft is designed with care, attention to detail, and a genuine love for these two remarkable destinations.
